Allegro Choir

A journey in song, both traditional and popular, from many places around the world, including some from our own land.
Allegro is an enthusiastic choir of men and women who enjoy singing together to create wonderful music, working from the philosophy that “Music is for All” and the wonderful enjoyment of singing great music in a friendly, focussed and self-disciplined group.
